Why Choose TWM

Dependable Service. Clean Results. Year-Round Care.

The Weedmen LLC provides professional lawn care, grounds maintenance, and snow management throughout the Milwaukee metro area. We serve homeowners, HOA communities, multi-building residential properties, and commercial sites that need dependable exterior maintenance without the stress of missed visits, inconsistent quality, or slow storm response.

Whether we're maintaining a residential yard, managing common areas, servicing a rental property, or keeping a commercial property clear and accessible, our goal is the same: reliable service, clean results, and fewer property issues for you to worry about. Everything we do is structured to protect your property, maintain curb appeal, and keep your grounds looking cared for year-round.

Dedicated Crew Assignments

The same crew services your property on every visit. They learn your layout, your priority zones, and the details that a rotating crew never would — which means consistent results instead of a different standard every week.

Documented Service Verification

Every visit is logged and time-stamped. You get a record you can pull up in seconds — proof of completion for your owners, your board, or your own files. No more taking a vendor's word for it.

Fully Insured & Bonded

General liability, commercial auto, and workers' compensation coverage on file. We'll provide a Certificate of Insurance and name your management company as additional insured before we ever set foot on your property.

Locked-In Contract Rates

Your rate is fixed for the entire contract term — no fuel surcharges, no peak-growth upcharges, no mid-season "adjustments." You can budget the year with one number.

Behind The Company

Warren Nelson, Founder

WN

Warren Nelson, Founder

The Weedmen LLC is led by Warren Nelson, a hands-on founder who takes pride in helping people feel confident about the way their property looks and functions. Warren understands that whether it is a family home, an HOA community, an apartment property, or a commercial site, the exterior is the first thing people see — and it should reflect care, consistency, and pride.

He started The Weedmen LLC to build a company people could depend on through every season: clean lawns in the summer, well-kept grounds in the spring and fall, and safe, accessible properties during Milwaukee winters. For Warren, the work is personal. Every property is treated with accountability, respect, and the goal of giving residents, owners, and property managers one less thing to worry about.
